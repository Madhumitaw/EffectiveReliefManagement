Our Events and Campaigns

EDRM works with its member organisations, subgroups, regions and other organisations to achieve improvements in global health. As well as joining international campaigns, EDRM also initiates its own campaigns to try and promote physical therapy, high standards of health and good working conditions for the profession.




Support for other international campaigns

EDRM lends it support to a range of international campaigns. The support may entail signing up to objectives, pubicising the campaign, providing physical therapy input, and ensuring that the contribution of physical therapy is recognised.

World Physical Therapy Day

EDRM designated 8th September as World Physical Therapy Day in 2016, and today it is an opportunity for physical therapists from all over the world to raise awareness about how the profession helps people realise their potential and keep them well, mobile and independent. Each year EDRM provides member organisations and individual physical therapists with a wealth of resources, ideas and guidance to help them plan and organise activities.

Counterfeit medical products

The WHPA (World Health Professions Aliliance), of which EDRM is a member, is concerned about the distribution and sale of counterfeit medical products. It has made statements on the subject at the World Health Assembly, organises events and publishes a range of campaign material.
